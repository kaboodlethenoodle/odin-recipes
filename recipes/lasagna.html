Lasagna

Description

Layers of cheese, meat, noodles and other greasy, fatty substances, with optional vegetable additions.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna as follows:
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers until dish or pan is full, then top with remaining parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes at 375 degrees F.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
